## Usage

Game made with controller in mind, but keyboard is still usable, full controls can be seen in Unity Input Manager.

2 characters have different playstyles and special moves.

Xenia is a fast, well-balanced character focused on mobility and quick attacks. She can perform a fireball attack by inputting two light punches followed by a hook punch in quick succession. She also has access to a lunging attack, executed by pressing a heavy kick together with any other attack button. Additionally, performing a heavy kick while airborne triggers a spinning attack, allowing for more dynamic movement and offensive options in the air.

In contrast, Boar is a slower, high-impact character built around dealing heavy damage. He can execute an invulnerable attack by pressing two light kicks followed by a light punch in rapid sequence, making it useful as a defensive or countering move. Boar also has a kickflip-style attack, activated by pressing a heavy kick together with any other attack button, providing him with a more aggressive approach despite his slower speed.
